I'm in San Francisco, the rest of the family is not, and Secret Santa isn't nearly as fun when there's no secret. Behold, the wonders of the interwebs!

Beyond just having to be in the same place, the picking is usually a bit of an ordeal, because we try to avoid certain pairings (like buying for oneself or immediate family members). My matching algorithm should get around all these problems:

  • People won't be assigned as "Santa" for themselves
  • Balancing between families is maximized
  • Exchanges between immediate family members are avoided if at all possible
  • No two people will exchange directly with eachother – there should always be at least one person in between
  • The passwords encrypt the exchange information, so it is impossible for anyone (even someone with admin access to the server) to find out who is "Santa-ing" for whom

Yes, I do realize this is kind of over-done. Yes, it is tacky. And yes, in case you had any doubts, I'm a bit of a geek.
